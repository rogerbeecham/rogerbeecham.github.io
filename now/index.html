What I’m doing now

Last updated

September 11, 2023

As of September 2023, I am focussing on:

  • Completing a book, Visualization for Social Data Science, to be published by CRC Press – link is to free development version.
  • Work as Acting Programme co-Lead on Consumer Analytics and Marketing Strategy (CAMS).
  • GISRUK2024, which I’m lead organising with some fantastic colleagues.
  • Turing Methods Challenge Fortnight, with Cagatay Turkay, and which funds a team of international experts on a dedicated research challenge, in this case the forking paths problem in data science.
  • Supervising two excellent PhD Researchers – Juan P. Fonseca-Zamora (w/ Robin Lovelace + James Tate), Juliana Novaes Bueno de Camargo (w/ Dan Birks + @robradburn) – and several follow-up projects with Caroline Tait (@troublestrife).
  • Teaching on GEOG5917, GEOG5009, GEOG5022, GEOG5967 and also Course 3D – Essex Summer School.
